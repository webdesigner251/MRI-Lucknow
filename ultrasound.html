Our Ultrasound Services Include:

A CT (Computed Tomography) scan is a fast and effective imaging method that provides detailed cross-sectional views of the body, helping doctors diagnose various conditions quickly and accurately.

  • Abdominal Ultrasound– Examines liver, gallbladder, kidneys, pancreas, and other organs.

  • Pelvic Ultrasound – Assesses reproductive organs, including uterus, ovaries, and bladder.

  • Pregnancy Ultrasound – Monitors fetal development and detects any abnormalities.

  • Musculoskeletal Ultrasound –Evaluates muscles, joints, tendons, and ligaments for injuries or disorders.

  • Doppler Ultrasound –Assesses blood flow in arteries and veins to detect blockages or vascular conditions.

  • Thyroid & Neck Ultrasound – Detects abnormalities in the thyroid, lymph nodes, and surrounding tissues.

  • Breast Ultrasound – Helps in the detection of cysts, tumors, or other breast conditions.

  • Prostate Ultrasound– Evaluates prostate size, structure, and detects abnormalities.

  • Soft Tissue Ultrasound– Identifies lumps, cysts, or infections in soft tissues.
